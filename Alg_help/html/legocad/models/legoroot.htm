LEGOCAD System

The LEGOCAD system is a modular simulation code concerning the dynamics modelling of complex systems.

Beginning from 1978, the LEGOCAD System bas been realized by ENEL/DSR/CRA (Italian National Electric Company - Research & Development Department - Automation Research Center).

The LEGOCAD includes a mathematical "core" for the numerical resolution of algebraical non-linear and differential equations in an iterative way, an user interface for process models assembling, a graphic interface to built regulation schemes and automation logics, and a graphic interface for the management and representation of simulations.

Integral part of LEGOCAD System is a series of libraries concerning thermodynamic and regulation modules whose purpose is the modelling of all components related to thermoelectric plants (process, logics and control automation).
