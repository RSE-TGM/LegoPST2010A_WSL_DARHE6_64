Test case 6: general description

The following test case represents a high pressure steam turbine of a conventional thermoelectrical power plant.

The turbine has been simulated with three blocks, representing an impulse turbine stage (block TURA - module STAZ), and two reaction stages (blocks STA1 and STA2 - module TREN).

At the outlet of each reaction stage a bleeding steam flow is delivered to a water preheater, but in this model only the two steam bleeding valve are simulated (blocks VAL1 and VAL2 - module SPIL).

The dynamical behaviour of this model has been studied in a case of a 30% load reduction.
