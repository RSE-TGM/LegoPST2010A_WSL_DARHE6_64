MODULE MIXN CARD A

Model Generalities

 

N-FLUIDS ENTHALPIC MIXER

 

The MIXN module simulates the perfect mixing of "N" fluids flowing into a cavity.

Simplifying hypotheses adopted in the physical-mathematical model definition are the following:

- absence of thermal exchange of the "N" fluids with the surface of the cavity;

- absence of phenomena of mass and energy accumulation in the cavity;

 

The used equations, both algebraical are:

1) the energy conservation equation

2) the mass conservation equation

 

The physical-mathematical model inside the module is not able to simulate in a satisfying manner cases of back-flow. If during the simulation of any transient the back-flow of any flows should happen (although simulation is not interrupted), the obtained quantity trends may be little reliable. So it is necessary to check that during the transient evolution this situation does not take place.

 

 

MODULE MIXN CARD B

Topological data

 

INPUT-OUTPUT SCHEME

 

N = number of input branches in the cavity (max. 10)

i = 1, 2, ... N

 

 

PHYSICAL MEANING OF MODULE INPUT-OUTPUT VARIABLES

 

[kg/s] WMIX flow rate of fluid flowing out of the cavity

[J/kg] HMIX enthalpy of fluid flowing out of the cavity

[kg/s] WE_i (i = 1, 2, ... N) flow rate of fluid flowing into the cavity from the i-th branch

[J/kg] HE_i (i = 1, 2, ... N) enthalpy of fluid flowing into the cavity from the i-th branch
