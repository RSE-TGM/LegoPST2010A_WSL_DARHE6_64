Valves and pumps

Click here to examine the theoretical documentation related to these modules

CPOM Centrifugal pump
The CPOM module simulates a subcooled water pump. The characteristic curve of the pump may be chosen in a set of standard components or assigned by the user using FORAUS utility (writing an auxiliary user FORTRAN routine).
It is used for every kind of pump, including extraction, circulating and feed pumps in steam electrical plants. It also may be used in non thermo-electrical plants (everywhere a pump has to be simulated).
This module belongs to the thermodynamic base library..

VALV Regulation valve
The VALV module simulates a water or steam valve. The characteristic curve of the valve may be chosen in a set of standard components or assigned by the user using FORAUS utility (writing an auxiliary user FORTRAN routine).
It is used for every kind of valve in electrical and non electrical plants (everywhere a valve has to be simulated).
This module belongs to the thermodynamic base library.

 

Water and steam cavities, manifolds and mixers

Click here to examine the theoretical documentation related to cavities, manifolds and mixers

Click here to examine the theoretical documentation related to feed water heaters

 

CAVE Cavity in thermodynamic equilibrium
The CAVE module simulates the dynamics of thermo-hydraulic phenomena in a cavity containing water and steam separated by the force of gravity effect, assuming the thermodynamic equilibrium of water and steam inside the cavity.
This module belongs to the thermodynamic base library, but it is no more used (TANK and CLAV modules are used instead of CAVE).

CLAV Non thermodynamic equilibrium cavity containing air, steam and water
The CLAV module simulates the dynamics of thermo-hydraulic phenomena in a cavity containing water, steam and air separated by the force of gravity effect.
The mass and energy storage in the liquid (water) and gaseous (air and steam) phases are simulated. As the module doesn't impose the thermodynamic equilibrium of liquid and gaseous phases inside the cavity, also the thermal exchange and the surfacial steam condensation (or water ebullition) are simulated.
The CLAV module is used to simulate steam drums of boiler (natural or forced circulation), flash tanks of once-through boilers, drainage collecting and expansion tanks, and other tanks usually containing water in liquid and steam phases.
This module belongs to the thermodynamic base library.
It is not yet documented.

COLT Water / steam manifold with metal wall
The COLT module simulates the mass and energy storage of subcooled water, saturated steam or superheated steam in a manifold.
Also the thermal behaviour of the manifold metal wall is simulated.
This module belongs to the thermodynamic base library.

MITN N inlets thermal mixer
The MITN module simulates the flow rates sum and the thermal equilibrium of many fluid flows (liquid or gaseous, with constant specific heat) in a pipe confluence (no storage effect is simulated).
This module belongs to the thermodynamic base library.

MIXN N inlets enthalpic mixer
The MIXN module simulates the flow rates sum and the enthalpic equilibrium of many water and/or steam flows in a pipe confluence (no storage effect is simulated).
This module belongs to the thermodynamic base library.

MVAM Surheated steam and methane mixer
The MVAM module simulates the flow rates sum and the thermal equilibrium of two flows, one of surheated steam flows and the other of methane (no storage effect is simulated).
This module belongs to the thermodynamic base library.

SERB Water reservoir
The SERB module simulates the water mass and thermal storage in a piezometric or pressurized reservoir (but not the thermal behaviour of the reservoir metal wall).
This module belongs to the thermodynamic base library.
It is not yet documented.

 

Water and steam pipes, exchangers and evaporators

Click here to examine the theoretical documentation related to these modules

TH2O Subcooled water pipe
The TH2O module simulates the thermo-dynamic behaviour of the fluid present in a subcooled water channel and the thermal behaviour of the metal wall of the channel. It is used for non boiling side of heat exchangers.
This module belongs to the thermodynamic base library.

TUBO Pipe head loss
The TUBO module simulates the head loss of the fluid present in an adiabatic pipe. No storage equation or thermal exchange model is present.
It is used to simulate head losses in plants pipes, when pipes volumes (and storage effects) are negligible (or accounted by inlet or outlet manifolds).
This module belongs to the thermodynamic base library.

 

Steam condensers, deareators and cooling towers

Click here to examine the theoretical documentation related to steam condensers

Click here to examine the theoretical documentation related to deareators

CNDN Steam condenser
The CNDN module simulates the thermo-dynamic behaviour of an water cooled steam condenser. The model simulates the mass and energy storage of condensing steam inside the condenser cavity and the thermal behaviour in the tube bundle, both for water and metal wall. Also auxiliary inlet flows are available for drainages and extraction pumps recirculating water.
This module belongs to the thermodynamic base library.

DEGN Deaerator with one pressure node
The DEGN module simulates the cavity and the dome of a steam deareator. The model simulates the mass and energy storage of water and steam inside both the deareator cavities (simplified model with only one steam pressure level).
This module belongs to the thermodynamic base library.

Flue gas side and metal wall of pipes and exchangers

Click here to examine the theoretical documentation related to these modules

FUMN N-cells flue gas duct
The FUMN module simulates the dynamics of the temperature of the flue gas in a cellized plain flue gas duct.
It is used to simulate the flue gas side of over-pass and back-pass in fossil fuel steam generators, of exchangers in heat recovery steam generators and of other heat exchangers.
This module belongs to the thermodynamic base library.

 

Steam turbines and related modules

Click here to examine the theoretical documentation related to these modules

TREN Reaction steam turbine stage
The TREN module simulates a reaction steam turbine stage (or group of stages). Usually the turbine is divided into groups of stages according to the bleedings positions. The TREN module doesn't simulate the turbine drainages.
This module belongs to the thermodynamic base library.

MART Kinetics of rotating masses
The MART module simulates the kinetic behaviour of rotating masses. It is used to evaluate the rotating speed of steam or gas turbine groups. For separate shaft combined cycle generators, only one MART module may simulate all the groups connected to the same electrical grid.
This module belongs to the thermodynamic base library.

 

Turbogas plants and related modules

Click here to examine the theoretical documentation related to these modules

BTGS Combustion chamber for Syngas-fed turbogas groups
The BTGS module simulates the thermal and chemical behaviour of a turbogas combustion chamber with bypass channel. The fuels which can be used are natural gas (mixed with DeNOx steam) and mixed syngas.
It does not simulate neither the mass and energy storage in the cold chamber and the hot chamber nor the head losses through the grid and the cooling channels, which have to be simulated with the library modules devoted to the simulation of compressed air systems (SERA, VAIN, CMSC, PORE). It is used (connected with CTGS, TTGS and FUGS) in order to realize a complete turbogas group model.
This module belongs to the thermodynamic base library.

CTGS Axial compressor for Syngas-fed turbogas groups
The CTGS module simulates the behaviour of an air axial compressor for turbogas group. The behaviour of the compressor is analysed stage by stage (or by groups of stages), in order to allow the correct simulation of air bleedings and blow-off valves. It is used (connected with BTGS, TTGS and FUGS) in order to realize a complete turbogas group model.
This module belongs to the thermodynamic base library.

FUGS Flue gas chemical composition for Syngas-fed turbogas groups
The BTGS module (simulating the combustion chamber for syngas-fed turbogas group) evaluates the chemical composition of the flue gases as mass fractions of the single chemical components, while the modules devoted to the simulation of flue gas side of exchangers (in heat recovery steam generators) have to read the composition of the flue gas in a different way.
The FUGS module performs the needed convertion. It is used (connected with BTGS, CTGS and TTGS) in order to realize a complete turbogas group model.
This module belongs to the thermodynamic base library.

MART Kinetics of rotating masses
The MART module simulates the kinetic behaviour of rotating masses. It is used to evaluate the rotating speed of steam or gas turbine groups. For separate shaft combined cycle generators, only one MART module may simulate all the groups connected to the same electrical grid.
This module belongs to the thermodynamic base library.

 

Air and flue gas system components

Click here to examine the theoretical documentation related to these modules

DAMP Flue gas damper
The DAMP module simulates a gas register (damper).
It is used to simulate both registers and head losses in the air / flue gas system of steam generators. As in this kind of ducts pressure is usually near atmospheric one, the DAMP uses simplified relations for fluid properties evaluation. Unfortunately, this causes that the module should not be used for the simulation of compressed air (or gas) subsystems.
This module belongs to the thermodynamic base library.

PLEN Gas plenum
The PLEN module simulates a gas manifold.
It is used in the air / flue gas system of steam generators. As in this kind of systems pressure is usually near atmospheric one, the PLEN uses simplified relations for fluid properties evaluation. Unfortunately, this causes that the module should not be used for the simulation of compressed air (or gas) subsystems.
This module belongs to the thermodynamic base library.

 

Compressed air and natural gas treatment

Click here to examine the theoretical documentation related to these modules

CMSC Air compressor with cooler and recirculation valve
The CMSC module simulates the thermo-dynamic behaviour of an industrial air compressor (volumetric, centrifugal or axial). Also the air cooling subsystem and the recirculation valve are simulated.
It is used (together with SERA and VAIN) to simulate compressed air systems. It may be used also for natural gas supply systems (with less accurate results).
This module belongs to the auxiliary systems library.

SERA Air reservoir with mass storage
The SERA module simulates the thermo-dynamic behaviour of a compressed air reservoir.
It is used (together with CMSC and VAIN) to simulate compressed air systems. It may be used also for natural gas supply systems (with less accurate results).
This module belongs to the auxiliary systems library.

VAIN Real gas valve
The VAIN module simulates the thermo-dynamic behaviour of a real gas valve.
Joule-Thompson effect is taken into account.
It is used (together with CMSC and SERA) to simulate compressed air systems. It may be used also for natural gas supply systems (with less accurate results).
This module belongs to the auxiliary systems library.

Auxiliary modules

Click here to examine the theoretical documentation related to these modules

DISN Distributor of an input variable to N output variables
The DISN module is used to make several copies of a variable.
It is used when a value (external input or other block output) must be connected with more than one input variables in the same block (user cannot connect more inputs of the same block to the same variable).
For external input distribution (when the value hasn't to be connected to more than one input in the same block) NODO module may be used instead of DISN.
This module belongs to the thermodynamic base library.

Electrical modules

AMME Simplified electrical load
The AMME module simulates a Simplified electrical load.
It belongs to the auxiliary systems library.

GENE Synchronous machine
The GEIN module represents a simplified model of a synchronous electrical generator.
This module belongs to the auxiliary systems library.

GIPO Motor joint simplified model for pump or fan
The GIPO module simulates the rotation dynamics of a joint between a motor and an operating machine (pump or fan).
This module belongs to the auxiliary systems library.

LINA Electrical line
The LINE module represents an electrical PI-line model.
It belongs to the auxiliary systems library.

MASI Asynchronous machine
The MASI module simulates an asynchronous three-phases motor.
It belongs to the auxiliary systems library.

RETE Power simplifield network
The RETE module simulates an isolated net equivalent.
It belongs to the standard base library.
It is not yet documented.

SBAR Electrical network node
The SBAR module simulates an electrical network node.
It belongs to the auxiliary systems library.

TRAF Transformer with tap changer
The TRAF module simulates a transformer with tap changer.
It belongs to the auxiliary systems library.
