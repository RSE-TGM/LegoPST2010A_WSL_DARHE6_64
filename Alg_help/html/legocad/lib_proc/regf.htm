MODULE REGF CARD A

Model Generalities

 

FREQUENCY REGULATOR

 

The module REGF simulates the behavior of a primary frequency regulator and of the turbine connected to it (the model output is, in facts, the mechanical power produced by the turbine).

The functioning is described by the following simplified equation:

 

s = Laplace operator

Kp = regulator gain (reciproque of the statism)

T1 = denominator time constant

T2 = numerator time constant

Omri= reference angular velocity

Omeg= net angular velocity

Qrif= reference power

Qmec= turbine mechanical power

 

The following relationship provides the turbine mechanical power:

 

1 + sT2

Qmec = Qrif + (Omri - Omeg) Kp -------

1 + sT1

In steady state:

Qmec = Qrif + Kp (Omri - Omeg) .

MODULE REGF CARD F

Residuals

 

RNI(1) =QPMG-QPM

Where:

If (SPAR=0):

QPM = QPRI

If (SPAR=1):

Steady state:

QPM = QPRI-KP*(OMEG-OMRI)

Transient:

QPM = QPRI - KP*K1*(OMEG-OMRI) + KP*K2*(OMEGP-OMRIP) + K3*(QPMP-QPRIP)

OMEGP=OMEG of previous step

OMRIP=OMRI of previous step

QPMP =QPM of previous step

QPRIP=QPRI of previous step

KP=1/Stat_%

K1=(DTINT+T2)/(DTINT+T1)

K2=T2/(DTINT+T1)

K3=T1/(DTINT+T1).
