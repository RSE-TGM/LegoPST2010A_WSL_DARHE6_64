Turbogas plants and related modules: the combustion chamber

 

The main features of the model are:

two control volumes, related to the cold chamber and the combustion hot chamber, in which the energy and mass accumulation phenomena are neglected;

two pressure nodes corresponding to the control volumes;

the fuel feed system dynamics is represented through a zero / pole transfer function;

the combustion delay is taken into account by means of a first order transfer function;

the thermal exchange between the hot chamber walls and the internal flue gas, as well as that one with the external environment are represented.

The combustion chamber is constituted by two control volumes: a cold chamber receiving the compressor air and a hot chamber in which the combustion takes place. The compressed air flows from the former to the latter through a grid, modelled as a pressure drop.
