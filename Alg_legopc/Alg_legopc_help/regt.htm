MODULE REGT

CARD A

Model Generalities

VOLTAGE REGULATOR

 

The module REGT simulates the behavior of an eccitation device made out of a voltage regulator and a converter. The pole-zero behavior is described by the following relationship:

 

                            1 + sT2

    Vecc = (Vrge - Vgen) Gv -------

                            1 + sT1

 

where:

s = Laplace operator

Gv = regulator gain

T1 = numerator time constant

T2 = denominator time constant

Vrge= generator reference voltage

Vgen= generator voltage

Vecc= eccitation device voltage

 

In steady state:

Vecc = (Vrge - Vgen) Gv .

MODULE REGT

CARD F 

Residuals

 

        RNI(1) = UF00-UFC

Where:

Steady state:

        UFC = UFRI-GV*(UMOD-URIF)

Transient:

        UFC = UFRI-GV*K1*(UMOD-URIF)+GV*K2*(UMODP-URIFP)+K3*(UFCP-UFRIP)

 

    UMODP=UMOD previous step

    UFCP =UFC previous step

    URIFP=URIF previous step

    UFRIP=UFRI previous step

    DTINT=time step

    K1=(DTINT+T2)/(DTINT+T1)

    K2=T2/(DTINT+T1)

    K3=T1/(DTINT+T1)
