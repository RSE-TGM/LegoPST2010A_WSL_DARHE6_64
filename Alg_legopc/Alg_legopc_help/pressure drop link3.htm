A wide spectrum of correlations able to calculate g in forced convection conditions is exploited by many LEGO modules. Those correlations consider ducts of whatsoever section (characterised by its hydraulic diameter) and for any given inclination with respect to the horizontal plane.

Those correlations don’t cover natural convection and pool boiling conditions.
